Data Marketing

Using data analysis and industry leading predictive tools, Pivot Developments thoroughly examines your customer data set, creating a framework for your company’s success, increasing response and ROI for your marketing programs. Pivot Development uses its S.O.S methodology to isolate and remove data "distractions", add data points, and produce model outputs that allow you to effectively select your most valuable targets and improve your direct marketing and outreach potential.

Pivot Developments utilizes analytical solutions which are fully scalable. Any size organization, from local retailers to national chains, can benefit from the knowledge Pivot Developments’ data analysts provide.

Pivot Developments works within all industry sectors including non-profit, retail, government, financial and media organizations.

Predictive Modeling

Pivot Developments’ predictive modeling solutions deliver highly efficient campaigns and help your company stay ahead of your competition. In addition to your data, Pivot Developments has access to third-party data sources that increase the depth of information about your prospects and customers.

Pivot Developments’ unique Data Integration and Processing techniques accurately and scientifically link data and individuals from various sources, bringing a highly granular set of data into our predictive modeling and analytics.

Pivot Developments’ predictive modeling solutions combine a strong intellectual knowledge of different industries with various approaches such as regression, statistics, neural networks, decision trees and other data mining tools. We exploit patterns of current and historic facts to make accurate predictions about the future.

Experience analysts provide comprehensive post-analyses and deep-dive evaluations of past campaigns to identify niche opportunities to further improve your campaigns. We recommend ways to improve the success of your campaigns by evaluating your target audience, your offers, and each users communication channel of choice.
