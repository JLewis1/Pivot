Data Cleansing

When your company data does not meet your demands, or when your data management projects are planned, it is an important time to ensure data quality and guarantee the Pivot Developments offers your company Data Cleansing services to ensure that your data loads are cleansed and enriched with additional information such cleansed postal code and address data. Pivot Developments’ data cleansing services will ensure that duplicate records are identified based on complex business rules.

With Pivot Developments’ Data Cleansing services we clean your data stocks according to your own rules while adhering to industry Best Practices. We work closely with you to identify conspicuous items and data inconsistencies from your processes and applications.

Features:
  • Data cleansing extracts customer data from the widest range of source systems, databases, files and applications
  • International address validation, correcting and normalizing addresses from 246 countries and territories using batch processing and on demand services
  • Error tolerant matching of address-specific elements against international reference data
  • Identification of duplicate records in customer data with powerful matching algorithms and country-specific knowledge bases, both in B2C and B2B fields.
  • Aggregation and consolidation of customer data to create a unified record
  • Enrichment of company master data with important supplementary information, such as GeoData and x-y coordinates
  • Transformation of data formats and dataset structures in a prescribed target format
  • Cleansed, matched, consolidated and transformed customer data is loaded into one or more target systems – with the focus on fast and efficient processing of mass data
Advantages:
  • Suitable for small cleansing projects with limited data quantities, as well for company-wide cleansing of very large data volumes
Success Factor:

Data cleansing is a major step in the data quality process, whether it is a question of cleansing old systems of master data in connection with migration projects, risk management, or for compliance.

Cleansed customer data is also of great importance for the realization of customer orientation with the priority of data use in operative, specialist fields, e.g., for ensuring customer loyalty, or in dialogue and direct marketing.

Data cleansing creates optimal data quality, offering an up-to-date, reliable and detailed level of data quality. Data cleansing thereby forms the basis for higher information content, good analysis results and effective business processes.

Data Management

With Pivot Developments’ Data Management you can integrate all of your company data throughout your entire company. A unified, high quality data warehouse enables quick analysis of all data available, thus enabling prompt actions and reactions to market changes and trends. Highest-possible data quality standards are the centre of Pivot Developments’ philosophy and service offerings. Pivot Developments’ data management not only allows your company to integrate data from multiple disparate or non-disparate data sources. Pivot Developments ensures that your data is of the highest quality and is conforming to the highest demands throughout your company. With this data quality as a foundation, you can confidently make the business decisions that you need.

Businesses today need to be able to react to customer wishes in a faster and more efficient manner. The need for integration of data from all systems and for a unified 360° view of customer profiles is becoming a requirement for all businesses. But in many companies, the IT organization is an ageing structure of heterogeneous IT systems which make this 360° view extremely difficult – or even impossible. Customer data needed for effective user profiling is distributed across multiple systems, with redundant data which dilutes the customer view. This disparate distribution of data makes it impossible for a unified user record to be created. Pivot Developments integrates disparate data sources across your company in order to create a unified customer view which will allow your company to be able to react quickly and efficiently to your customers’ needs.

Pivot Developments’ professional services help you to transition your company’s data smoothly and free of redundant entries into a unified data repository hosted either within your IT infrastructure or hosted with a Tier 1 Provider. The consolidation of data requires that all data is complete, free of errors and no redundant items exist. If data quality in the differing source systems is sub-standard, this will have a negative effect on data quality in the unified data repository, and as such on the results of the Business Intelligence (BI) analysis.

Pivot Developments consolidates data sets from all systems involved – company-wide. Using error-tolerant matching algorithms it can identify similar and identical datasets. Using this data as a basis, your company can create the perfect golden records and thus a 360° customer view.

With Pivot Developments your company is able to keep its operative activities running efficiently. You can prepare analytic evaluations and organize further planning, and thanks to optimized data quality you can use new applications faster and more effectively. You keep within your budget limits and punctually comply with the schedule of your data project.

Without effective data management, a company can be presented with various challenges:
  • Multiple heterogeneous data sources and different data structures
  • Redundant items in master data, systems and beyond
  • Data sources have differing quality levels
  • Complicated metadata administration
  • Redundant items cause incorrect matching
  • Creation of a golden record (and thereby a 360° customer view) is not possible
Benefits from data management solutions from Pivot Developments:
  • Properly formatted and cleaned data, as dictated by business needs for effective data management
  • Support from a team of experienced data experts during the entire project
  • Cost reduction with standardized methods and proven best practice models
  • Permanent transparency with your company and full documentation of all services rendered
  • Optimized data quality for efficient and profitable work
  • Implementation of the Unified Customer Record principle, taking into account that different users have different demands on the data
